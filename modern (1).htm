Modern Calligraphy

  • The art of calligraphy, using traditional Copperplate Calligraphy materials
  • Which are a pen nib, a pen nib holder, and ink
  • The artist does not stick to the traditional Copperplate calligraphy style but adds his or her's own embelishments
  • This leads to much more creativity and a more unique calligraphy community.
